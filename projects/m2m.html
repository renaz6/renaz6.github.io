Mission to Mars




A concept of an exhibit about NASA's Mars missions. Created with projections in a model box. Mission to Mars takes guest on a journey to learn about Mars and the future of the planet. Structure of exhibit: Introduction, Timeline of past Mars exploration and future planned endeavors, Summary of the planet, What astronauts/ life on Mars might look like.
Role: Projection Designer, Art Director
