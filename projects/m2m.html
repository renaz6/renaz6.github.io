Mission to Mars

Role: Projection Designer, Art Director
Special Thanks to Sven Ortel
Tools/Software: Photoshop, Illustrator, Vectorworks, QLab

Concept

To document NASA's Mars Missions in an exhibit that strikes a balance between a traditional museum experience and elaborate technology.
I decided that to display so much information in a small space, I had to create four differently "themed" areas that used different strategies to capture the guests' attention.
As for color and design, I wanted to choose a pallette that was subtly evocative of space travel, Mars, and patriotism.




Planning & Diagrams

Since I decided to only use rear-projection, one of the challenges I had was finding locations to house the projectors. My solution to this was to build "projection closets," which would have one wall as the rear-projection screen so that the projectors could be out of sight for the exhibit.




Execution

The final presentation was done using a 1/2 inch model box of the exhibition space. Full video coming soon.
