Seymour the Cannibal




A play about a vagrant named Jeremiah Pith who throws extravagant dinner parties to scout out his next victims.
Role: Assistant Director
Playwrite: David Rhoads
Directors: Heather Barnwell & Serena Zamarripa
University of Texas Theatre Guild
Photographer: Marc Cornejo
As the AD, I had a heavy hand in the creative process of the play, from holding 10+ hours of rehearsal every week to casting, blocking, and directing actors.
